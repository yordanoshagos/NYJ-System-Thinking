Payment Question

Your mother pays you 12 GHS per hour to babysit your sister, Tachema, on weekends. You offer your brother 10% of your weekend salary to cover Saturday night from 9 p.m. to 3 a.m.

  • Maximum hours she can work on the weekend: 48 hrs
  • Total earned: 48 × 12 GHS = 576 GHS
  • Offer to brother: 10% of 576 GHS = 57.6 GHS
  • Amount she keeps: 576 - 57.6 = 518.4 GHS
  • Total payment to brother and his wife: 57.6 GHS

Estimate of Daily YouTube Video Views

YouTube has over 2.7 billion monthly active users. Assuming 1.75 billion users daily, each watching 3–4 videos:

  • Videos per day: 3 × 1.75 billion = 5.25 billion
  • Average video length: 9 minutes
  • Watch time per person: 27 mins/day = 0.45 hours
  • Total watch hours: 1.75 billion × 0.45 = 787.5 million hours/day

Adopting New Technology at Work

Steps to handle an unfamiliar technology in a new project:

  1. Be open-minded and willing to learn
  2. Research deeply: functions, pros, cons
  3. Ask questions to peers or experts
  4. Identify similarities with known tools
  5. Practice with small projects
  6. Experiment and iterate
  7. Seek guidance and resources
  8. Create and share documentation

Handling a Product Failure on Launch Day

If a critical feature fails during a conference demo:

  • Identify and assess the root cause
  • Communicate with PR, execs, and marketing
  • Highlight other working features
  • Acknowledge the issue transparently
  • Prioritize fixing and share a resolution timeline
